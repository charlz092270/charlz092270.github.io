Welcome to Bethlehem

Bethlehem, the Christmas City1, is a city in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. As of 2022, the city's population was 77,617. It was incorporated on July 17, 1917. It is located in the Lehigh Valley region of the state and is classified as urban. The average income level of residents is $61,786 as of 2022. 2
