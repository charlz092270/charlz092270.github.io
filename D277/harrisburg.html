Welcome to Harrisburg

Harrisburg is the capital of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. As of 2022, the city's population was 50,183. It was incorporated on April 20, 1860. It is located in the Central region of the state and is classified as urban. The average income level of residents is $44,276 as of 2022. 1
