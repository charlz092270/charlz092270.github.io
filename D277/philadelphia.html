Welcome to Philadelphia

Philadelphia is the largest city in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. As of 2022, the city's population was 1,567,258. It is located in the Eastern region of the state and is classified as urban. The average income level of residents is $56,517 as of 2022.1 It was founded on October 27, 1682.2
